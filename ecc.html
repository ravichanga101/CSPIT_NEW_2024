Energy Conservation Cell

CHARUSAT - Empowering Sustainability Through Innovation

Our Objectives

Promote Energy Conservation

Spread awareness about energy-saving measures and their importance.

Workshops & Seminars

Engage students and staff in interactive energy-saving activities and discussions.

Non-Conventional Energy

Maximize energy generation using renewable sources and promote sustainable practices.

Our Activities

Solar Power Plant Initiative

Total capacity of 990 KW installed at CHARUSAT campus.

Annual Poster-Making Competition

Energy Conservation Cell organized poster making competition on National Energy Conservation Day .

Energy Conservation Day Play

A play was performed by students of the Electrical Engineering Department to raise awareness about National Energy Conservation Day.

Energy Conservation Workshops

Organized annually for CHARUSAT Stakeholders aiming to increase understanding of energy conservation and energy-saving techniques.

Solar Workshop for Students

Conducted to create awareness among students about solar panel-based projects focusing on the development and use of solar technologies.

LED Lighting Initiatives

LED Lighting Initiatives for replacement of conventional lighting with LED technology in classrooms.
